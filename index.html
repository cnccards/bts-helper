How Hit Probability is Calculated
Pulling live from MLB Stats API:
• Each team's top 10 batters by season AVG (min 20 ABs)
• Career batter vs pitcher (BvP) record
• Ballpark hit factor
• Starting pitcher ERA/WHIP/BAA
Formula:
Season AVG blended with BvP (40% weight if 10+ ABs, 20% if 5-9 ABs) × park factor. Converted from per-AB probability to per-game (4 AB) probability.
Double Down: Only when BOTH picks are 68%+. If either is under 65%, go single pick.
Still avoid:
✗ Batters we show but you notice are slumping (0-for-20)
✗ Pitchers with ERA under 2.50 regardless
✗ Never-start lineups (check scratches at game time)
🛡 Streak Saver activates at 10-game streak automatically.
